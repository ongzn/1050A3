Introduction

Located in the south eastern suburbs of Melbourne, Clayton Monash Motor Inn & Serviced Apartments is conveniently located 500 metres from Monash University and within walking distance to the Monash Medical Centre.

Clayton Monash prides itself on setting new standards, provides our guests with complimentary free parking, in room internet access with wireless high speed connectivity.

What's Nearby

Our accommodation are conveniently located 20 min from Melbourne CBD, with easy access to transportation, offering direct routes to the city and Chadstone Shopping Centre. For travellers arriving or departing by plane, our airport shuttle bus service provides convenient pick up and drop off.
